# CDAD operating limits
Guardrail #1
AI must not modify L0 files.

Guardrail #2
AI must not change the architectural style.

Guardrail #3
AI must not introduce a new paradigm without approval.

Guardrail #4
AI must not replace core frameworks without approval.

Guardrail #5
Architecture changes require ADR or explicit approval.

Guardrail #6
Business rules require human review.

Guardrail #7
AI must read governed context before implementation.
